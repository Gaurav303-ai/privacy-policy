Introduction

TruckMint ("we", "our", or "us") is a transport and fleet management platform designed to help truck owners and drivers manage trips, expenses, settlements, and operational records.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use TruckMint.

By using TruckMint, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Business Analytics Disclaimer

TruckMint is a transport management platform that provides business insights based solely on information entered by users.
  • We do not independently track, monitor, verify, or audit your business activities.
  • All analytics, reports, summaries, profit/loss calculations, and operational insights are generated only from information voluntarily entered by users.
  • Users are responsible for the accuracy of the information they provide.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We never collect the following
  • Current or background location data
  • Device contacts
  • SMS messages or call logs
  • Advertising identifiers (GAID / IDFA)
  • Microphone or camera data beyond optional uploads
  • Any data not explicitly listed in Section 2

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share only when

  • Required by law or a valid legal process.
  • Necessary to protect our legal rights.
  • With our service providers (Firebase and Cloudinary) solely for operating the application — bound by their own privacy policies.
9

Data Security

  • We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorised systems only.
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
10

Data Retention

  • We retain your information for as long as your account remains active.
  • Data may also be retained as required for operational or legal purposes.
  • Uploaded documents and records may remain stored until your account is deleted.
  • Upon verified account deletion, all associated data is permanently erased within 30 days.
11

Account Deletion

You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  • Send an email to truckmint.support@gmail.com
  • Include your registered email address
  • Include your registered mobile number
  • Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. After deletion, all personal data, trip records, expense records, and uploaded documents are permanently removed from our servers.

Children's Privacy

  • TruckMint is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.
  • If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your TruckMint account and all associated personal data at any time, in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

To exercise this right, please use one of the following methods:

Email Request
Send an email to truckmint.support@gmail.com with subject: "Account Deletion Request" including your registered email and phone number.
Online Form
Visit our Contact Us page and submit a deletion request through the Account Deletion form.

⏱ Processing Time: All verified deletion requests will be processed within 30 days of receipt. Upon completion, your account, profile, trip records, expense data, uploaded documents, and all associated personal information will be permanently and irreversibly erased from our servers.
